Early Voting Is Underway!

May 22, 2025

The Democratic Party of Virginia will have a state-administered primary to determine their nominees for candidates to be on the November 4, 2025 General Election ballot.

Vote early at the City of Salem Registrar's Office (19 East Clay Street).

Monday - Friday / May 2 - June 13, 2025

8am - 12:30pm / 1:30pm - 5pm

Saturday, June 7 / Saturday, June 14, 2025

9am - 5pm

Please see the Salem City Registrar's Office website for a sample ballot and additional information about voting in Salem City.
