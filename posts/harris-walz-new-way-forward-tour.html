Team Harris-Walz New Way Forward Tour

Sept 18, 2024

On the heels of Vice President Harris' decisive debate victory Tuesday night against Donald Trump, Team Harris-Walz kicked off the New Way Forward tour that will hit every media market in every battleground state over four days to mobilize support, drive enthusiasm, and reach the voters who will decide this election.

At back-to-back rallies in Charlotte and Greensboro, North Carolina, Vice President Harris doubled down on her message that the country should turn the page from Trump and instead pursue a new way forward under her leadership.

She took aim at Donald Trump's dangerous and extreme Project 2025 agenda, which was on full display on the debate stage, forcefully calling Donald Trump out for his embarrassing answer that his "concepts of a plan" about how to end the Affordable Care Act justify ripping health care away from tens of millions of Americans.

Vice President Harris - along with Governor Walz, Second Gentleman Emhoff, Mrs. Walz, and a roster of her top surrogates - will be on the trail again to emphasize the choice in the election that Vice President Harris' debate performance made clear: going forward with Kamala Harris, or going backward to the past with Donald Trump.
