Working hard in Salem for our democracy

Nov 5, 2024

This past week has been one of hard work and late nights.

SWVA for Harris-Walz - Nov 2, 2024

Special thanks to Samuel Lumpkin, President of Roanoke College Democrats, Dominic Harden, Co-President of Roanoke Young Democrats, Sarah Goodman with Red Wine and Blue, Chairwoman Susan Swecker, Lily Franklin, L. H. Hamlar Event Center, folks with Roanoke City Democratic Committee, Roanoke County Democratic Committee, Montgomery County VA Democrats, Democratic Committee of Botetourt, Roanoke Valley Democratic Women, and our whole team at Salem City Democratic Committee for a day focused on protecting our country from the hate-filled rhetoric that invaded our city today.

When we vote, we win!

Signs at the Civic Center - Nov 4, 2024

It's easy to feel overwhelmed and alone with the negativity MAGA brings.

Salem City Democratic Committee members met at Salem Civic Center to place over 400 signs for democratic candidates. Our presence here in Salem won't be drowned out by all of the hatred and lies. Thanks to the random young democrats who drove up and helped!

Poll workers and observers - Nov 5, 2024

Salem City Democratic Committee members and volunteers are hard at work today at the polls. Your continued dedication to protecting democracy is a feat to behold.
